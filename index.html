❓ How to Play

Multiplication problems fall from the sky. Answer them before they hit the ground!

  • Swipe left/right (mobile) or use ← → arrow keys to switch target
  • Type the answer and press Enter or Fire! — on mobile, just say the answer aloud
  • You have 3 lives — lose one each time a problem hits the bottom (Normal mode)
  • Answer fast for bonus points — streaks of 3× and 5× multiply your score
  • Catch the ❄️ Freeze item to slow all falling problems for 5s
  • Catch the 💚 Life Up item to regain a lost life
  • Every 5 levels a Boss appears — defeat it for 50 bonus points
  • A dot-grid hint appears after you miss the same question several times
  • Questions you get wrong come back more often — so you'll master them
  • The game gets faster each level — it's a high-score challenge, not a race to the finish!

🎤 Voice input

  • Normal mode (default): say the answer — it submits automatically (e.g. "forty-eight" or "48")
  • Trigger mode: say your trigger word then the answer (e.g. "fire 48") — only that combination fires; a bare number just previews it. Enable above and set your preferred word.
  • fire / shoot / go / yes — submit the answer currently shown, or use as the trigger word prefix
  • next / forward — switch to the next problem
  • back / previous — switch to the previous problem
  • clear / delete — clear the answer box
